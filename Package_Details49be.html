Package Overview

Day 1: Arrival in Pokhara night halt
Day 2: Pokhara to Jomsom by flight, Muktinath Darshan & halt
Day 3: Jomsom to Pokhara by flight, Pokhara site seening halt
Day 4: Return back

Destination Details :
