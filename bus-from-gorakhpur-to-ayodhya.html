How to get from Gorakhpur to Ayodhya

When you want to get from Gorakhpur to Ayodhya, you have a few options to consider. Traveling between these destinations is possible by different means of transport:

  • Bus
  • Train
  • Taxi

If you’re looking for a quick trip, it’s recommended that you use Bus. However, if you’d rather take your time with a slower, more affordable travel option, you can book a Bus ticket.

The cost of travel will depend on the means of transport you choose for your trip. A Bus ticket is the most expensive option - it will cost you about €45. If you want to save on transportation, it’s better to take a bus as a bus ticket costs as low as 350 INR .

How far is Gorakhpur from Ayodhya?

If you’re traveling by land, it’s important to know the distance from Gorakhpur to Ayodhya. Your trip will be a total of 132 km.

How long does it take to get from Gorakhpur to Ayodhya?

The travel time from Gorakhpur to Ayodhya can vary depending on the mode of transportation you choose. All things considered, the whole journey should take from 2 to 3 hours.

How much does it cost to get from Gorakhpur to Ayodhya?

The cost of the trip from Gorakhpur to Ayodhya varies according to what means of transport you choose for your journey. The most affordable operator is Kesherwani Travels : travelling by bus expect to pay as low as 350 INR for your ticket.

However, if you’re after more upscale travel . The most expensive variant is to opt for a Bus - a one-way Bus ticket can cost as much as 350 INR.

Which means of transport is the best for the route?

Buses

Taking a bus from Gorakhpur to Ayodhya is cheaper than flying. Quite often, a long-distance bus is the cheapest transportation option. Buses normally make bathroom and/or lunch stops along the way.

Things to remember before opting for a bus ride:
  • If possible, make sure to choose higher-class buses for a more comfortable travel experience. Such buses often have an air conditioning system, soft reclining seats, Wi-Fi, bathrooms, etc.
  • As with plane tickets, buses are better booked in advance so that you can secure a good seat.
  • While booking a bus, keep in mind that delays are possible. Yet it still pays to get to the bus station at least 15 minutes prior to the scheduled departure.
Taxi

Catching a cab is the easiest way to get from Gorakhpur to Ayodhya. It allows you to tailor-make your trip, plan all the detours and see all the places you want. You can choose the type and the size of your car as well to be as comfortable as you'd like to.

Depending on the route, it might cost as much as 3000 INR.

Things to remember: It's a good idea to follow the route on Google Maps or any other GPS navigator.

Trains
Things to remember while choosing a train ticket:

Consider everything carefully while choosing the class of your train travel. In most cases, the second class is comfortable enough – it features 2 or 4 berths in each compartment and is often air-conditioned.

If you’re in for a premium experience, consider getting a first class ticket. The price of first class tickets may include Wifi, and a choice of snacks and drinks..
